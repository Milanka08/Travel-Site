Mirissa  

 Mirissa, a coastal paradise in Sri Lanka, enchants visitors with its stunning beach, captivating whale watching experiences, secluded Secret Beach, breathtaking Coconut Tree Hill, vibrant Mirissa Harbour, picturesque Parrot Rock, delectable seafood restaurants, and thrilling surfing opportunities.

 Seegiriya

 Sigiriya, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Sri Lanka, is a marvel of ancient engineering and natural beauty. Its towering rock fortress, adorned with intricate frescoes, offers breathtaking panoramic views. The impressive Lion Rock, surrounded by lush gardens and water gardens, is a testament to the ingenuity of the ancient kingdom. The Mirror Wall reveals ancient inscriptions, while the Sigiriya Frescoes showcase vibrant paintings of celestial maidens. Sigiriya is a treasure trove of history, art, and awe-inspiring vistas that captivate every visitor.

Vesak  

 Vesak festivals celebrate the life and teachings of Gautama Buddha, the founder of Buddhism. These joyous occasions involve various rituals, such as bathing Buddha statues, giving alms to monks, and lighting colorful lanterns. Devotees come together to reflect on the virtues of compassion, generosity, and mindfulness, creating a spiritually uplifting atmosphere.

 Sinhala and Tamil new year

 Sinhala and Tamil New Year, also known as "Aluth Avurudu" in Sinhala and "Puthandu" in Tamil, is a vibrant cultural festival celebrated in Sri Lanka. It marks the beginning of the traditional New Year according to the Sinhala and Tamil calendars. The festivities include various rituals, customs, and joyful gatherings, where families come together to engage in traditional games, prepare special meals, and exchange gifts. It is a time of renewal, harmony, and gratitude, as people seek blessings for a prosperous year ahead while embracing their cultural heritage.
